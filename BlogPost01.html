My Introduction and Journey into The Tech Field

Monday, September 13th 2021

My journey into Tech/Coding began when I was laid off from Chicago Public Schools as a Teacher of College & Career. A friend of mine named Gino heard of what happned to me and reached out to me to see if I would be interested in starting a starting a career in Coding/Tech Field

I remember Gino saying "Ok, at first you will feel overwhelmed withe language. But continue and do not give up. I will be here to assist you if you need me. First begin with Codecademy."

The first week of Codecademy was exactly that, overwhelming. As a former Teacher with a Bacholors in Education, I did not understand the language. I continued however to see how far I could go. After taking my time and three weeks later I was so proud of myself for completing the Codecademy program.

Codecademy was a great introduction to HTML. I learned about the anatomy of HTML. I was not aware of openning & closing tags. There are structues in place when working with HTML such as The Body, Headings, DIVS, and Attributes.

In HTML I learned how to link to other web pages, adding tables, rows, table headings, styling with CSS, inputting forms, adding labels, passwords, input numbers, range input, and check box input.
